Main Characters

Meet Shinchan, his family, and his best friends!

Shinnosuke "Shinchan" Nohara

The energetic and mischievous five-year-old who loves snacks, action heroes, and making everyone laugh.

Himawari Nohara

Shinchan's adorable baby sister who loves shiny jewelry and handsome boys.

Misae Nohara

Shinchan's caring but strict mother who is always trying to keep the family under control.

Hiroshi Nohara

The hardworking father of the family who enjoys relaxing after work despite Shinchan's daily adventures.

Toru Kazama

The smartest member of the Kasukabe Defense Force and Shinchan's responsible best friend.

Nene Sakurada

A cheerful girl who enjoys playing house but has a funny temper when things go wrong.

Masao Sato

A shy but kind-hearted member of Shinchan's group who is easily frightened.

Bo-chan

A quiet and mysterious boy known for his calm personality and his always-runny nose.

Ai Suotome

A wealthy, confident girl who develops a big crush on Shinchan despite his silly behavior.

Shiro

The Nohara family's intelligent and loyal pet dog who often helps Shinchan in unexpected ways.
